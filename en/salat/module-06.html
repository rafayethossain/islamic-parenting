Module 6

Little Qari

5 Short Surahs: The Noor-filled part of prayer

Tajwid Key:
  • j = Normal 'J' sound.
  • z = Soft 'Z' sound (as in: ذ).
  • zw = Heavy 'Z' sound (as in: ض).
  • q = Deep 'Q' sound from the throat (as in: ق).
  • - = Sign for elongation (long vowel).

Lesson 6.1: Surah Al-Fatiha (A Conversation with Allah)

This is the most important Surah of the Quran. It teaches us how to praise Allah and how to ask Him for help.

Iyya-ka na‘budu wa iyya-ka nasta‘een. (You alone we worship and from You alone we ask for help.)

Lesson 6.2: Surah Al-Ikhlas (The Identity of Allah)

This Surah teaches us that Allah is only One. He has no parents or children. He is the Creator of everything in the world.

Qul huwallahu ahad. (Say, He is Allah, [who is] One.)

Allahus-Samad. (Allah, the Eternal Refuge.)

Fun Fact for Kids: "Did you know? The Prophet (PBUH) said that if you read this Surah 3 times, you get the reward of finishing the entire Quran!"

Lesson 6.3: Surah Al-Kawthar (The Shortest Surah)

Kawthar is the name of a spring in Jannah. For those who love Allah, He has kept this special gift in Jannah.

Inna- a‘tayna-kal Kawthar. (Indeed, We have granted you Al-Kawthar.)

Lesson 6.4: Surah Al-Asr (The Importance of Time)

Time is very valuable. Those who believe and do good deeds are the real winners.

Wal-‘Asr. Innal insa-na lafee khusr. (By time! Indeed mankind is in loss.)

Lesson 6.5: Surah An-Nas (The Shield of Protection)

This Surah keeps us away from all kinds of bad thoughts and the whispers of Shaitan. It's like an 'Invisible Shield' around us.

Qul a‘u-zu birabbin-nas. (Say, I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind.)
