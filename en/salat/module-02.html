Module 2

The Royal Invitation

Adhan: The invitation to come to Allah's Palace

Tajwid Key:
  • j = Normal 'J' sound.
  • z = Soft 'Z' sound (as in: ذ).
  • zw = Heavy 'Z' sound (as in: ض).
  • q = Deep 'Q' sound from the throat (as in: ق).
  • - = Sign for elongation (long vowel).

Lesson 2.1: What is Adhan? (A Royal Invitation from Allah)

1. Core Spiritual Concept

Imagine if the king of your country invited you to his palace, how happy would you be? Adhan is an even bigger invitation, from the King of kings, Almighty Allah, specifically for you. Whenever you hear the Adhan, understand that Allah is calling you to His court.

2. Words & Meaning of Adhan

Arabic Transliteration Meaning
اللهُ أَكْبَرُ Alla-hu Akbar Allah is the Greatest.
أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللهُ Ash-hadu al-la- ila-ha illalla-h I bear witness that there is no god but Allah.
أَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا رَسُولُ اللهِ Ash-hadu anna Muhammadar Rasululla-h I bear witness that Muhammad (PBUH) is the Messenger of Allah.
حَيَّ عَلَى الصَّلَاةِ Hayya 'alas-Salah Come to prayer.
حَيَّ عَلَى الْفَلَاحِ Hayya 'alal-Falah Come to success.
Fun Fact for Kids: "Did you know? Shaitan runs away when he hears the sweet sound of Adhan! Adhan protects our home like a fortress from Shaitan."

Lesson 2.2: Responding to Adhan (Answering a Friend's Call)

1. Core Spiritual Concept

When someone calls our name, do we stay silent? Of course not! Similarly, responding to Adhan is like saying "Yes" to Allah's call. We repeat what the Muadhin says. However, during 'Hayya 'alas-Salah' and 'Hayya 'alal-Falah', we say:

لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّা بِاللَّهِ

Transliteration: La- hawla wa la- quwwata illa- billa-h.

Meaning: "There is no power or might except with Allah's help."

Pro-Tip for Parents: "Stop talking or working as soon as the Adhan begins. This will teach your child that Allah's call is more important than all worldly affairs. Make a short dua together after the Adhan ends."

Lesson 2.3: Iqamah (The Sweet Signal for Prayer to Begin)

1. Core Spiritual Concept

Iqamah is the signal just before the prayer starts. Adhan calls people to prayer, and Iqamah says—"Now everyone stand in line, our conversation with our beloved Allah is about to begin!"

2. Special Phrase of Iqamah

قَدْ قَامَتِ الصَّلَاةُ

Transliteration: Qad qa-matis-Salah.

Meaning: "The prayer has started."

Lesson 2.4: The Story of Bilal (RA)'s First Adhan

Story: Long ago, when Makkah was conquered, the Prophet (PBUH) told his dear friend Bilal (RA) to climb onto the roof of the Kaaba and give the Adhan. Bilal (RA) had a very sweet voice. People's hearts were filled with joy hearing his Adhan. Bilal (RA) was the first Muadhin of Islam, whom we still remember with love today.
