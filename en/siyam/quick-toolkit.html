🌟 5 Manners of Iftar

Here are the beautiful Sunnahs to follow at the time of Iftar:

1
Hasten Iftar: Start Iftar as soon as the Adhan is called without delay. This is our Prophet's Sunnah.
2
Make Dua: Raise your hands and talk to Allah just before Iftar. This is a time when Duas are accepted.
3
Dates & Water: Start with dates if possible, otherwise plain water. It gives the body quick energy.
4
Say Bismillah: Don't forget to say "Bismillah" before the first bite or sip.
5
No Wasting: Only take as much as you can eat. Allah does not like wasting food.
